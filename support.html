Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How do I create a new note?
Click the "New Note" button in the top bar on the home page. You can then choose to record audio, upload documents (including audio files), add a web link, or create a note manually. Your first note is completely free with full access to all features.
Can I organize my notes in folders?
Yes! Click "Create Folder" to add a new folder. You can create nested folders for better organization. Click on a folder to navigate into it. This helps you keep your study materials organized by subject, course, or topic.
How does the search function work?
Type your search query in the search bar. The search looks through all your notes, even across different folders. It searches note titles, content, and summaries. Clear the search to return to normal view.
What is the free tier?
You can create and use one (1) note completely free with full access to all features, including all study modes, AI features, document processing, and premium features. After creating your first note, you'll need to subscribe to create additional notes or continue using the Service.
What study modes are available?
RocketLearn offers five powerful study modes: AI Summary (instant summaries of your content), Feynman Technique (learn by teaching), Smart Flashcards (auto-generated with spaced repetition), Interactive Quizzes (multiple choice with instant feedback), and Practice Exercises (hands-on practice). All modes are available for your first free note.

Audio Recording and Transcription

How do I record audio?
Go to "New Note" → "Record audio". Press the microphone button to start recording, and press it again to stop. Your audio will be automatically transcribed using AI technology. The transcription appears in your note content.
What audio formats are supported?
We support MP3, WAV, and other common audio formats. You can upload audio files via the "Upload documents" option or record directly using your device microphone. The AI will automatically transcribe the audio content.
How accurate is the audio transcription?
Our AI transcription service uses advanced speech recognition technology. Accuracy depends on audio quality, background noise, and speaking clarity. For best results, record in a quiet environment and speak clearly. You can always edit the transcription after it's generated.
Can I transcribe video audio?
Yes, you can upload video files and the audio will be extracted and transcribed. Supported video formats include MP4, MOV, and other common formats. The transcription process may take longer for longer videos.

Documents and File Uploads

What file types can I upload?
You can upload PDFs, Word documents (.doc, .docx), OpenDocument files (.odt, .ods), Excel files (.xls, .xlsx), PowerPoint (.ppt, .pptx), text files (.txt, .md, .json), images, audio, and video files. The AI will extract and process the content from all supported document formats.
Is there a file size limit?
Yes, each file should be under 10MB for best performance. You can upload up to 3 files at once per note. For larger files, consider splitting them into smaller parts or compressing them before uploading.
Can I upload files from cloud storage?
You can add web links to Google Drive files and other cloud storage services. However, you may need to download the file first and then upload it to RocketLearn for processing. We're working on direct cloud storage integration for future updates.
What happens to my uploaded files?
Your files are securely stored in our cloud storage and are only accessible to you. Files are encrypted at rest and in transit. You can download your files at any time, and they remain available as long as your account is active.

Subscriptions and Billing

How much does a subscription cost?
Subscription pricing is displayed within the application and may vary by region. We offer monthly and annual subscription plans. Check the subscription page in the app for current pricing in your area.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings in the app or by contacting us. Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period. You'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of your paid period.
Can I get a refund?
Refund policies depend on the platform through which you purchased your subscription (Apple App Store, Google Play Store, or direct purchase). For subscriptions purchased through app stores, refunds are handled by the respective store according to their policies. For direct purchases, refunds may be available within 14 days of purchase, subject to applicable law.
Will my subscription auto-renew?
Yes, subscriptions automatically renew unless you cancel before the renewal date. You'll be charged automatically at the end of each billing period. You can manage your subscription and turn off auto-renewal in your account settings or through the app store where you purchased the subscription.
What happens if I don't renew my subscription?
If your subscription expires, you'll lose access to premium features and won't be able to create new notes beyond your first free note. Your existing notes and data will remain stored, but you'll need to resubscribe to access all features and create additional notes.

AI Features

How does the AI chat work?
The AI chat allows you to ask questions about your notes and study materials. Simply type your question, and the AI will provide answers based on the content in your notes. The AI understands context from your uploaded documents and can help explain concepts, answer questions, and provide additional insights.
Are AI-generated summaries accurate?
AI-generated summaries are created using advanced language models and are generally accurate. However, you should always review and verify AI-generated content, especially for academic or professional use. The summaries are meant to assist your learning, not replace your own understanding and review of the material.
Can I edit AI-generated content?
Yes, you can edit AI-generated summaries, flashcards, and other content. The AI-generated content is provided as a starting point, and you're encouraged to customize it to better suit your learning needs.

Technical Issues

The app is not loading or is slow. What should I do?
Try the following: check your internet connection, close and restart the app, clear the app cache (mobile) or browser cache (web), ensure you're using the latest version of the app, and check if our service status page shows any known issues. If problems persist, contact support.
I'm having trouble uploading files. What's wrong?
Ensure your file is under 10MB, check that the file format is supported, verify your internet connection is stable, and try uploading one file at a time. If the issue continues, try a different file or contact support with details about the error message you're seeing.
My audio transcription failed. What can I do?
Audio transcription may fail if the audio quality is poor, the file is corrupted, or the file format is not fully supported. Try re-recording in a quieter environment, ensure the audio file is not corrupted, or try converting the audio to MP3 or WAV format. If the problem persists, contact support.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
On the login screen, click "Forgot Password" or "Reset Password". Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Check your email (including spam folder) and follow the instructions to create a new password.
Can I use RocketLearn offline?
RocketLearn requires an internet connection to sync your data, process files, and generate AI content. However, you can view previously loaded notes and content while offline. For full functionality, an active internet connection is required.

Account and Privacy

How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account through the Settings page in the app. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Please note that deleting your account will permanently delete all your notes, documents, and data. This action cannot be undone. Make sure to download any important content before deleting your account.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we take data security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. We use industry-standard security measures, including secure authentication, access controls, and regular security audits. Your data is only accessible to you and is protected by Row Level Security policies.
Can I export my notes?
Yes, you can download your documents and export your note content. Use the download feature for individual documents, or copy your note content to export it. We're working on a bulk export feature for future updates.
Who can see my notes?
Only you can see your notes. Your data is private and secure. We do not share your content with other users or third parties except as necessary to provide the service (such as processing through AI services) and as described in our Privacy Policy.
